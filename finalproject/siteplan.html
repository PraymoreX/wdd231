MedTech Career Explorer Site Plan

WDD 231 Individual Project

Site Name

MedTech Career Explorer

The name was chosen because it clearly describes a website that helps users explore careers that combine healthcare and technology.

Site Purpose

MedTech Career Explorer helps students and professionals discover careers that connect medicine and technology. Users can learn about career paths, salary ranges, educational requirements, required skills, and employers within the healthcare technology industry.

Scenarios

  • What healthcare technology careers are available for someone interested in both medicine and technology?
  • What education and skills are needed to become a Biomedical Engineer?
  • Which MedTech careers have the highest salary potential?
  • How can I compare different healthcare technology careers before selecting one?

Color Scheme

Deep Navy Blue (#001F4D) – Used for the header, footer, navigation, section headings, and major background areas.


Teal (#00D1C7) – Used for buttons, icons, links, hover effects, and accent elements.


Light Gray (#F5F5F5) – Used for page backgrounds and content sections.

Typography

Poppins

Used for headings, navigation items, hero section titles, and card headings.

Open Sans

Used for body text, career descriptions, labels, and general content.
